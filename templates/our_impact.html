Creating a Positive Impact

Legacy is committed to using our capabilities to create a positive impact on the environment and society. As we grow, so does the scale of the impact we can make.

Through Legacy Foundation, we implement transformative projects in Education, Women's Empowerment, and Sustainable Urbanization to create a positive impact on the environment and society. We are recognized in global sustainability indices and rankings for the work we do to address the environmental impact of the built environment.

Environmental Impact

Our approach to sustainable construction focuses on reducing carbon emissions (decarbonization) and on creating assets that will be resilient to future climate change (resilience).

Our decarbonization initiatives are designed to lead the transition to a low-carbon future for the real estate industry.

Our resilience initiatives focus on water resilience, biodiversity, and asset safety.

The Legacy Net Zero Urban Accelerator is a pioneering platform in partnership with a US-based think tank to develop sustainable solutions.
